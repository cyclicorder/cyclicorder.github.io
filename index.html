I investigate geometric and structural properties of vision-language embedding spaces — equivalence structures, semantic misalignment, and representation vulnerability — and their implications for the safety of large multimodal models. Current work targets adversarial attack pipelines for BLIP-2, LLaVA-1.5, and Qwen-VL.
